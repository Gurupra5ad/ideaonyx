Our Mission

"Thinkers think and doers do"We, Team MeChonyX aim at organizing robotics workshop at school and college level for engineering graduates, junior college and school students targeting maximum benefits to the enthusiasts attending the workshop, The workshops are designed to help students develop their knowledge and skills, and to also provide a real time industry exposure dedicatedly delivered by us.
Our Workshops have a fixed duration and study structure which is efficiently delivered to the attendants, our objective is to help students gain an overall knowledge of the applied technology and it's functioning, for present and future reference we provide ample materials in physical and electronic format focusing the subject and application areas of robotics technology, we empower students with real innovation thereby enabling the creation of knowledge which in turn shall accelerate our environment with knowledge based interlinked eco system, we possess the materials and skills in the learning space to make learning extensive in a very limited duration, our focus is on innovation and improving the learning curve through hands-on workshops.
The benifits include...

  • Hands-on training on specific areas of robotic development
  • Real time implementations through practical sessions
  • Multipurpose project based trainer kits
  • Well versed study materials and guides
  • Valid Certification
  • Project Assistance
  • Internship Opportunities and Placement assistance
We aim in serving as a safe and fun robotic workspace for students to pursue their interest in robotics.
. We are currently providing robotic workshops in schools and colleges around Chennai, and hoping to develop it to more centers all around India in the coming years.
